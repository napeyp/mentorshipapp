Nape Mentoring

Nape YP Mentorship is a year-round virtual mentoring program created among Earth and space science organizations to facilitate the development of student and early career professionals

Join As
Mentors are professionals who guide and advise mentees in their career paths to help them succeed. A mentor's role is to inspire, encourage, and support their mentees.
Mentees are students or early career professionals who want guidance and advice to further their careers and to be successful. Mentees can expect to strengthen and build their networks, and gain the skills and confidence necessary to excel.
